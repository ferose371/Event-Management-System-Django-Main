EVENTS

WEDDING EVENT

A wedding event is a special celebration of the union of two individuals in marriage. It typically involves a variety of elements such as ceremony, reception, food, music, decorations, and attire.

PARTY EVENT

A party event is a social gathering of people for the purpose of celebration and enjoyment. It can range from small, intimate gatherings to large, elaborate affairs, and may include food, drinks, music, games, and other forms of entertainment.

TECHNICAL EVENT

A technical event is a type of gathering that focuses on showcasing the latest advancements and innovations in technology. It typically includes demonstrations, presentations, workshops, and exhibitions related to various fields.
